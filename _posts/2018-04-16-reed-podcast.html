In this podcast, Jonathan Teubner discusses power and authority in the Trump presidency with Isaac Reed, Professor of Sociology at the University of Virginia. We discuss the performative nature of Trump's political speeches and focus on his use of "binary." We look closely at President Trump's speeches at the World Economic Forum and the UN General Assembly and at his response to the events of August 11th and 12th in Charlottesville.
